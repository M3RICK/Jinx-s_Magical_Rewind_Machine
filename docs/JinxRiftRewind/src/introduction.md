# Introduction

Welcome to **RiftRewind** - where League of Legends players level up their gameplay with personalized AI coaching and community-driven improvement.

## What is RiftRewind?

RiftRewind is a next-generation player improvement platform designed to replace traditional stat-tracking sites like OP.GG and DPM.LOL. We go beyond just showing you numbers... We provide **actionable insights**, **personalized coaching**, and a **fun, engaging experience** that makes getting better at League actually enjoyable.

Simply enter your summoner name#tag and region, choose your vibe (Coach Mode or Roast Mode), and dive into a world of personalized feedback and coaching.

## What Makes Us Different?

Unlike traditional stat sites, RiftRewind offers:

- **General Use Of AI Tools** - This site was aspiring to really be personal to the player, so a lot of things have been generated to fit your gameplay and style
- **Interactive Visual Feedback** - Explore your gameplay on an interactive map with targeted advice for each lane and objective
- **AI-Powered Personal Coaching** - Chat with your personal AI coach powered by Claude Sonnet 4.5, who remembers your progress across sessions
- **Shareable Player Cards** - Show off your unique playstyle with beautiful, AI customized cards featuring your favorite champions
- **Two Modes of Feedback** - Choose between constructive coaching or hilarious roasts that will still be constructive (we do judge)
